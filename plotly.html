Violence Trend by Country

Column

Violent Rate Across Years

merged_violence_df |> 
  plot_ly(x = ~as.factor(year), 
          y = ~violence_rate, 
          color = ~country,
          type = 'scatter', 
          mode = 'lines', 
          colors = "viridis",
          line = list(width = 1)) |> 
  layout(title = "Violence Rates Across Years", 
         xaxis = list(title = "Year"),
         yaxis = list(title = "Rate (per 100,000 population)"))
